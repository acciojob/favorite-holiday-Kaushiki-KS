Independence Day


"Independence Day" redirects here. For other uses, see Independence Day (disambiguation). For patriotic days not necessarily linked to independence, see National day. An independence day is an annual event commemorating the anniversary of a nation's independence or statehood, usually after ceasing to be a group or part of another nation or state, or after the end of a military occupation, or after a major change in government. Many countries commemorate their independence from a colonial empire.
